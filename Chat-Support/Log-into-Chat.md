![image.png](/.attachments/image-ca3a6575-a3fd-4ff4-b48b-816e75251cba.png)

3. A new browser will appear to set your desired settings. 

![image.png](/.attachments/image-f2a44ee7-6123-4f6e-aac6-b1e747f40173.png)
